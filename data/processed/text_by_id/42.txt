Equality, Diversity and Inclusion Champions Equality, Diversity and Inclusion Champions support our commitment to equality, diversity and inclusion across campus. Our Equality, Diveristy and Inclusion (EDI) Champions are situated across each of our Schools and Professional Services divisions. The champions disseminate information and best practice, raise awareness of equality and diversity issues, encourage involvement in initiatives and help promote a culture where staff and students are treated fairly and with dignity and respect. You can read more about the role of the EDI Champions [PDF 99KB].
