Report + Support The University of Nottingham is committed to ensuring we do everything we can to provide a safe and supportive environment for everyone. Our campuses are a pleasant and inclusive place to study and work but, as with any community, issues can arise. The University does not tolerate bullying, harassment, hate or sexual misconduct on our campuses. If you, or someone you know, has experienced or witnessed an incident like this, you can tell us anonymously or provide your contact details through our Report and Support system. By making a disclosure through the system you are not making an official report or complaint to the University, it is to enable you access to support and help you make an informed decision. However if what you have told us raises serious safety concerns about you or others, we may need to take action even if you haven't asked us to. Do you have a question about Report and Support? Our FAQs may be able to help. If you are a student and require support completing this form please speak to the Support and Wellbeing team (click on 'I need some support') who can help you with this. We are also offering drop in sessions for students who may prefer to meet with a team member privately before completing a form. Our next drop ins are 5th May 2026 11am - 1pm 19th May 2026 1pm - 3pm at the Wellbeing Hub, C Floor, Portland Building (next to Student’s Union Advice) . Please note only pre-arranged appointments are available outside of these dates and times.
